How our soaps are made:

Method:

1. Mix water and lye
2. Set aside to cool
3. Melt oils, set aside to cool
4. Blend lye water and oils to form a soap “batter”
5. Pour into mold and let harden for a day
6. Turn out of the mold, cut into bars and let cure for 2-3 weeks.
